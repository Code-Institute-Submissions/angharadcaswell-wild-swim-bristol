Social

Join a supportive and inclusive community. Make friends with like minded people and enjoy time in nature with them. There are loads of fantastic social groups in Bristol offering meet ups, advice and just a lovely way to connect with new people.

Mental health

There is growing evidence of the benefits of wild swimming for your mental health. The immersion of your body in cold water increases your dopamine levels. Focusing on your breathing while you enter cold water is a mindful experience and makes you feel present. Along with many other mental health benefits.

Fitness

Swimming can be any level of a workout. From powering out lengths to a more gentle stroke just to get moving a little. It is low impact unlike running and uses lots of muscles.

Post-swim Buzz

Technically, this should come under mental health benefits. It is such a joy, it needs it's own section. Every wild swimmer will be able to tell you about their post-swim buzz. It's the reason we all keep coming back! Even if you took some convincing to get in the water, it's pretty much guanteed you will be giddy after your swim. Its such a great way to start the day or wash away the days stress!
